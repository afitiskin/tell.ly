Everything you need for the phone communication

  • Use your own greeting

    Use your own greeting to be heard by everyone who calls you. You can play a greeting first then transfer the call to the secretary.

  • Record all your calls

    You can record any call during conversation with one click. Or you can do settings to record and store all calls of your employees.

  • Analyze your calls

    Statistics for all your calls is presented in a chart form. You can use it for better understanding of your customers and market, to evaluate the effectiveness of advertising campaigns, to reduce communication costs.

  • Common address book

    You can share contacts within your team. Administrator can see all contacts so information won't lost if employee is left

  • Use any phone or even a browser

    You can use IP phones, SIP applications and our own browser based phone to make and receive calls. Also you can forward all income calls to your mobile or land line.

  • Personal settings for employees

    Each employee can upload his own voicemail greeting, define working hours and set call forwarding to any external number.

  • Voice to text conversion

    All recorded calls and voicemails are presented in text form. That's kinda cool.

  • Skype integration

    Link your Skype account and Tell.ly to forward all income calls from phone number to Skype or other way round.

  • Text messaging

    You can send text messages from your web account. You can schedule the time of sending and do mass messaging.

  • Music on hold

    You can upload your own melody to play it while caller is waiting for an answer.

  • Call history

    All calls are logged. Each employee has his own call history. There are incoming, outgoing and missed calls, voicemails and text messages. Recorded calls are available to listen.

  • Toll-free and local numbers

    Pick out your local or toll-free numbers. You can have one or several numbers.

  • Working hours

    Working hours of your company is when someone can answer the call. Set this period and then the out of hours you can play special greeting or transfer call to the voicemail.

  • Multiple lines

    You won't miss a call from your clients because we provide you multiple lines.
