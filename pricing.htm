Questions

Is there a setup fee?

No. There is no setup fee for any of our plans.

Do I need a credit card to sign up?

Yes, we require a credit card to sign up. We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover.

Can I cancel my account at any time?

Yes. Although we would hate to see you go, you can cancel your account from your account control panel.

Can I change my plan later on?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time with no additional handling charges.

If I cancel my account within 30 days of signing up, will I be charged any fees?
